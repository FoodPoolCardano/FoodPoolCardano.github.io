Our Setup

We want to focus our stake pool to be reliable, secure, environmental friendly and decentralized.

  • Monitoring

    We monitor our pool 24/7 365 days to make sure to have a best possible uptime.

  • Hardware

    3x RaspberryPi 4 8GB incl. 240GB M.2 SSD of which one is the block producer and two are relais.

    Second relay to go live soon!

  • Power Supply

    Our pool runs on low-energy devices and is powered by green energy only. For additional redundancy we are planning to integrate a battery pack and mobile internet for the very rare case of a power outage.

  • Network

    We run the the stake pool inside our home in a metropolitan area in Germany. Our 50 M/bit network is provided by one of the most reliable ISPs in the country.

  • Security

    We follow the security recommendations by IOHK to make our pool secure.

    Anything unanswered? Feel free to reach out on twitter or drop us an email. We will get back to you as soon as we can.
